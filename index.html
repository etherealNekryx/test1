These are my three elevator pitches.


The Commander Creator

For Magic: The Gathering players who want to build commander decks around a new legendary creature that doesn't actually exist, the Commander Creator is a semi-random card generator that builds an entirely new legendary creature for you and your friends to builds fun decks around. Unlike basic card generators, our product lets you select specific pieces of the card you wish to generate to create the perfect new commander to run your jank deck.


Random DnD Generator

For Dungeons and Dragons players who want to create items or NPCs on the fly, the Random DnD Generator is a simple program that will gereate random names and abilities for magical items or NPCs. Unlike some other random generator, our product is simple and easy to use.


Speedrun Clock

For video game speedrunners who want a simple and adaptable program to track thier objective time, the Speedrun Clock is a adaptable timer that allows you to manually enter a number of objectives that will keep track of your time. Unlike other speedrunning timers, our product is super simple and straightforward, allowing multiple objective to be timed and recorded at the push of a button.
